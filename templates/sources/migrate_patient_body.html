Tidepool recently improved how your clinician and their clinic use our software, and as a part of these updates we want to keep you informed of how you can control who can see your Tidepool data.

Here’s how sharing has been updated:

  • The Tidepool account {{ .LegacyClinicianName }} has now become part of the {{ .ClinicName }} Tidepool Clinic Account.
  • Moving forward, any Tidepool clinician user associated with {{ .ClinicName }} will be able to see your Tidepool data.

Here’s what you can do:

  • If you want to continue to share your data with {{ .ClinicName }}, no action is needed from you.
  • If you wish to stop sharing your data with {{ .ClinicName }}, log in to your Tidepool account, click Share, and for the Clinic account you want to no longer want to have access to your data, click the three dot menu and click "Remove clinic".

As always, you remain in control of who has access to your data.

Please contact support@tidepool.org if you have any questions or concerns.
